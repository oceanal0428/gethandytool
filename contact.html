Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you.

Whether you have a question about one of our calculators, found a bug, want to suggest a new tool, or are interested in partnership or advertising opportunities, our team is happy to help. We read every message and do our best to respond within 2–3 business days.

Email

General Inquiries
contact@gethandytool.com

What to Include

To help us respond quickly, please include as much of the following as applies to your message:

  • Subject line that summarizes your inquiry (e.g., "Tip Calculator Bug", "Currency Request", "Partnership").
  • The page or tool you're referring to, with the URL if possible.
  • Device and browser (e.g., iPhone / Safari, Windows / Chrome) for technical issues.
  • Screenshots if you're reporting a visual or layout problem.

Topics We Handle

Bug Reports & Feedback

Something not working as expected? Saw a math mistake? Let us know. User reports are the fastest way for us to find and fix issues.

Tool Suggestions

HandyTool is growing. If there's a calculator or utility you wish existed, email us with your idea — especially if you'd use it regularly yourself. We prioritize requests that come from real use cases.

Advertising & Partnerships

Interested in sponsored placement, affiliate partnership, or custom integrations? Include your company name, website, and a short description of what you have in mind.

Press & Media

Journalists and content creators covering personal finance, travel, or consumer topics are welcome to reach out for comment, data, or to use our calculators in stories.

Privacy & Legal

For privacy-related requests (GDPR, CCPA, data deletion) or legal notices, please clearly mark your subject line with "Privacy Request" or "Legal Notice" so it reaches the right person quickly. See our Privacy Policy for more details on your rights.

Response Time

We typically reply within 2–3 business days. During holidays or high-volume periods, responses may take a little longer — thank you for your patience. Messages that appear to be spam, unsolicited bulk offers, or SEO link-building pitches may not receive a reply.

Please Note

HandyTool provides informational calculators only. We cannot offer personalized financial, tax, or legal advice via email. For professional guidance specific to your situation, please consult a qualified advisor.
